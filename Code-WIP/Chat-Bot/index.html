About Us

We, Team Octave developed a AI Chat Bot for shopping from E-commerce websites to make shopping through internet easy and efficent to search for products and view details about the products without logging in to the website

  • Our Bot can perform searches for the desired product
  • The Full required details of the product we choose is
  • Filters can be selected by the users

Our AI ChatBot is developed using Open Source Rasa which is implemented using python.And we managed to fetch data from Amazon using Webscrapping with Module Beautiful Soup.You can view our github Repo Below.

Commands and What you can ask to Baymax

All commands you can perform and how to ask it.

  • 01 You can Search for products

    You can search for any products you wanna know about by providing BayMax the name of the product.(Ex: i need to buy laptops, i wanna buy gaming chair)

  • 02You can get more Details about products

    After searching for products, BayMax will show you Top 3 products on the category you searched for.You can just have you ask BayMax to tell you more about the product with its serial number.

  • 03 Daily Deals

    Just like searching for products, youi can just ask BayMax to show you Daily Deals. So BayMax will provide you with best Deals Available today.

  • 04Filters and Top Brands

    You can ask BayMax to show you filters and brands, and Bot will provide you available options and you can select any options with corect serial number
